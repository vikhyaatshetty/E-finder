About E-Finder

Who is E-Finder?

E-Finder is an Web Application that helps you find your nearest electric charging station at ease. Apart from searching, you can enjoy many features lika Pre-booking, Recharge offers, Live navigation & More.

With its web platform, E-Finder helps all the electric car drivers and future EV drivers to quickly find answers to their questions.

E-Finder was launched in 2021 as a special project of group 32 to help electric car drivers find public charging stations in the India

The E-Finder project rapidly grew into a leading website to help EV drivers charge anywhere.. At E-Finder we work on a daily basis to develop and refine our offer to the EV community.

From finding charging stations to providing market reports to industry and governments, we offer a variety of services.

Mission

The main goal of our company is to help everyone make informed choices when it comes to electric transportation

Help

Help electric vehicle drivers across India find a charging station, plan their trips, interact with the community and activate charging stations.

Simplify

Simplify the complete process before, during and after the purchase of an electric car by offering an all-in-one solution. E-Finder provides all the necessary information an EV driver needs.

Encourage

Encourage electric vehicle adoption and the deployment of a user friendly EV charging infrastructure. E-Finder helps drivers and key players in the electric vehicle industry stay up-to-date by providing the most accurate information on the industry.
